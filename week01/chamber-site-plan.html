Site Name

Name: Santiago of Chile Chamber of Commerce

Reason: People often say "Santiago is Chile" because the capital hosts the best businesses and top companies. The name represents the city where the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

Our purpose is to promote local businesses and foster their ties to the community. In addition to showing the community what the chamber of commerce is all about, supporting its members and representing their interests.

Scenarios

  1. How can a business become a member of the Santiago Chamber?
  2. What are the benefits of membership?
  3. Which are the partner companies?
